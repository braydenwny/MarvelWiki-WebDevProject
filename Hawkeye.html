Creation

 Hawkeye was created by Stan Lee and Don Heck, and was originallya villain, however eventually joins the Avengers

Origins

 Clint Barton was orphaned at 6 years old, and after joining a traveling carnival, he was taken under the wing of other members The Swordsman and Trickshot, and was trained to be a master Archer. After seeing Iron Man in action he was inspired to don a costume. After a few slip ups, being accused of theft, and falling for Soviet Spy Black Widow, he eventually joined the Avengers and becomes a hero.

Different versions of Hawkeye
