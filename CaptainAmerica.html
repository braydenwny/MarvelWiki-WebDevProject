Origins

 Captain America's first appearance is in Captain America Comics #1 by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby at Timely Comics - The predecessor of Marvel Comics. Captain America is usually depicted as Steve Rogers, a Super Soldier who would fight against the axis powers in World War 2, and eventually, was frozen and revived in modern day. He goes on to be a founding member of the avengers.

Different versions of Captain America

Different variations include:
  • Steve Rogers, Captain America - Earth 616
  • Steve Rogers, Captain America - Ultimate Universe
  • Chris Evans Steve Rogers/Captain America - MCU
  • Sam Wilson, The Falcon/New Captain America - Comics
  • Sam Wilson, The Falcon/New Captain America - MCU
  • Peggy Carter/Captain Carter
