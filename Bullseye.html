Creation

 Bullseye's first appearance was in Daredevil #131 in March 1976, and was created by Marv Wolfman and John Romits Sr.

Origins

 Bullseye's real name is unknown, however he goes by the name Benjamin Poindexter, and occasionally by the name Lester. He is a supervillain who has no superpowers, but has the ability to use a variety of object as lethal projectiles, ranging from shuriken to playing cards. He is an enemy of Daredevil, Hawkeye, and the Punisher. He has high homicidal tendencies and a personal vendetta against Daredevil

Different versions of Bullseye

Different variations include:

  • Colin Farrel - Bullseye 2003 Daredevil film
