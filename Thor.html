Creation

  Debutting in Journey into Mystery #83, Thor was created by Jack Kirby, Stan Lee and Larry Lieber, and was based off of the Norse god Thor.

Origins

 Deciding Thor needs to be taught a lession in humility, his father Odin decides to send Thor to Earth with no memories of being himself, but rather being a mortal, medical student named Donald Blake. After witnessing aliens, Blake accidentally finds Thor's hammer Mjolnir and transforms back into the god Thor. After defeating the aliens, Thor is able to live a double life, fighting aliens as Thor, and helping people medically as Donald Blake. That is until his brother and rival Loki discovers this, and decides to keep sending enemies and illusions. Thor eventually becomes a founding member of the Avengers, along side Hulk, Iron Man, Ant-Man and the Wasp. Thor continues to live on Earth for the most part and defend it.
