Creation

 Black Widow, or Natasha Romanoff was created by Stan Lee, Don Rico, and Don Heck. Her debut was in Tales of Suspense issue 52 in April 1964. She was originally introduced as an enemy of Iron man and a Russian spy but later defected from Russia to join American intelligence agency S.H.I.E.L.D., as well as a member of the Avengers.

Origins

 Born in Stalingrad, Natasha Romanoff was a orphaned child who after being brought to a sinister place called the Red Room, was trained as a Soviet spy. Planted with false memories to ensure loyalty she was sent to infiltrate and manipulate information from Tony Stark, which led to her battling his alter-ego Iron Man. She later recruits Hawkeye and battles Iron Man once more. Eventually she joins the Avengers, has adventures with other heroes such as Daredevil and Spider-Man, and becomes an agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. under Nick Fury.
