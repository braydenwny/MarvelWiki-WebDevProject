Creation

 Iron man was created Stan Lee, Larry Leiber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. Originally the Iron Man Comics were a way to have Stan Lee explore the idea of the Cold War in Comics, however throughout time he has been transitioned to more modern war and technology themes.

Origins

 Anthony Edward Stark, or Tony Stark, is a rich scientist/philanthropist/inventor who gets kidnapped in hopes of forcing him to create a weapon of mass destruction. During the kidnapping he suffers a severe injury in his chest, and after getting materials for their weapon, he instead makes himself a suit of armor to save his life and escape. After escaping he continues to develop the suit in different designs, and after keeping his identity hidden for a while, he eventually reveals his identity to the public as Iron Man, even becoing a founding member of the Avengers.

Different versions of Iron Man

Different variations include:

  • Tony Stark - Ultimate Universe
