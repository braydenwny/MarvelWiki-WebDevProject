Creation

 Red skull is an alias used by a couple different characters in the Captain America series. The earliest is a man named George Maxon, which appeared in Timely Comics Captain America Comics # 1. However the main version of the character that is most popular is Johann Schmidt, which appeard in Captain America Comics # 7 in October 1941 and created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby.

Origins

  Johann Schmidt was a Nazi agent and worked closely under Adolf Hitler, he is the Arch nemesis of Captain America. Originally his red skujll was just a mask but evcentually he suffers horribly and becomes disfigured to which it matches his name. He was affiliated with both the Nazis and the fictional HYDRA agency.
