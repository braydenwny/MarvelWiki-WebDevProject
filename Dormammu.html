Creation

 Dormammu's first appearance was in Strange Tales # 126 in November 1964 by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko

Origins

 Dormammu is an ancient magic creature who rules over the Dark Dimension, and is the arch nemesis of Dr. Strange. At first he tries to take over the universe, however after Strange helps him fight a different threat he promises Strange to never come back to Earth. Indebted to Strange he keeps this promise, but constantly finds loopholes to keep persuing power, such as getting human henchment to do his bidding
